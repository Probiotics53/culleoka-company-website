Our Story

Transforming the cleaning experience by eliminating harmful effects and harnessing the power of nature's own cleaning mechanisms.

The Beginning

Culleoka Company was born from a simple belief: cleaning shouldn't compromise your family's health or the environment. Founded in Culleoka, Tennessee, we set out to eliminate the toxicity generally associated with traditional cleaning products.

Our journey began with a revolutionary discovery - the power of beneficial bacteria. While others were adding harsh chemicals, we looked to nature's own cleaning mechanisms: probiotics that work continuously to break down organic matter and protect surfaces.

Tennessee Headquarters Image
Innovation Lab Image

Innovation Through Science

We didn't just want to create another "green" cleaner - we wanted to create something better. Our team of scientists and researchers spent years perfecting probiotic formulations that actually outperform traditional chemical cleaners.

The result? Products that continue working for up to 96 hours after application, creating healthier environments while being completely safe for your family, pets, and the planet.

Our Mission

To transform the cleaning industry by providing innovative, non-toxic, and eco-friendly solutions that harness the power of beneficial bacteria. We're committed to creating products that protect both your family's health and our planet's future.

"Less Toxins, More Life" isn't just our tagline - it's our promise to every family that chooses Cully's.
